Character Availability: Can't Stop Crying DLC, own a copy of BattleBlock Theater on the same account, or purchased in Insane Store

Hatty Hattington is a character from one of the Behemoth's other games, BattleBlock Theater. His entire moveset is a reference to his own game, but this caused his character to favor fashion over function. His splash attack comes out incredibly late, just so it can show the Golden Whale from his own game.
