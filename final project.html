      In case you do not know what Castle Crashers is, it is a 2008 hack n'slash made by the Behemoth. It has a variety of pets, playable characters and weapons. The main story is a relatively short game, but with online minigames and insane mode, there is much more content to be had. The plot is simple; an evil wizard stole the magical crystal and kidnapped the princesses, and you need to kill everyone in your way to defeat the wizard. In online mode, you can play special arena modes, such as treasure hunter. In that gamemode, each time you dig up treasure you're opponent loses health. It is a very interesting gamemode, because it is near impossible to damage the opponent directly with your shovel. There are many other modes such as archery and beefy mode. The other minigame is different depending on whether you play the original version or the remastered version. In the original version you can play All you Can Quaff, which is a very basic mashing game. On the remastered version you play back-off barbarian, and in it you and other the other players are on a gridded playing field trying to not get crushed by a barbarian. Every 10 seconds another barbarian spawns and you have to keep outrunning them. Every time you press any of the wasd keys on keyboard or the xyab buttons on the xbox controller you move one space up down left or right on the grid. However, each space you move has a different control scheme so w may move left on one tile but up on another. This will often times end up with you accidentally running into the barbarians and losing.
